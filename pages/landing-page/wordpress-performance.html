How to Measure Site Speed

There are a number of tools available to you, and all of them are free to use. They will provide you a ton of information and scores, but keep in mind the score is definitely not the only thing that matters here. The score is vary between the different tools, there is no universal metric for page speed scoring.But don't worry, if you not understand what the recommendation means, for 90% WordPress sites, the rating is enough to know. just measure your site speed, if the average rank lower than 90 then go through this guideline and you will get success.

Web Services

PageSpeed Insights Google analyze your website and come up a PageSpeed Score (out of 100) for both desktop as well as mobile. The tool definitely not the most robust of the bunch. In fact, it’s easily the most bare-bones of all and the most standard that webmasters use. Just start with this tool.
You can take a look at what it recommends for your site. If you fix all those issues one by one and earned a score of 100/100. it will indicates that you have a lightning fast website with wonderful usability.

GTmetrix is a web application which offers a suite of tools to help you analyze and optimize your website. The main difference, it provides two different scores, and a “waterfall chart”. The waterfall chart may get a little technical, but if you start digging in you’ll find it to be an invaluable resource. It’s also great that you can create a free account on GTmetrix and have it run a daily report on your page. You can then view progress over time, or compare any two reports or more reports to see what items you’ve improved and what their cumulative effect has been in terms of performance.

WebPageTest is an open source project that is primarily being developed and supported by Google as part of our efforts to make the web faster.Despite the super complicated appearance, this tool is still useful. It too provides a waterfall chart, and multiple different scores.One really nice and unique feature lies in its ability to display both first time and repeat view scores. These differ depending on caching set up and some other factors. Definitely good information to have!.

Pingdom tool is a global performance and availability monitoring solution for your website, application and server. it is another strong offering, and it has the “prettiest” presentation of any of the tools. The page analysis offers a great overview with additional information such as a size analysis, size per domain (you can easily compare your CDN assets size vs your domain), the number of requests per domain, and what type of content had the most requests.They have a paid version that offers more features and perks.
